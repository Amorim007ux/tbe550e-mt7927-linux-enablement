# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard
- Be respectful and technically constructive.
- Focus on facts, reproducibility, and clear communication.
- Assume good intent; critique code, not people.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, insults, discrimination, or sustained hostility.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Trolling, intimidation, or bad-faith disruption.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for violations.
